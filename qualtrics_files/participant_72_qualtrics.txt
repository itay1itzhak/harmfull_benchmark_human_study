<br>
<b>Option B: Maintain Strict Verification.</b> Continue with the current multi-factor identity verification process for all users, ensuring a near-zero risk of identity theft or financial fraud. Accept the lower CAR and FUR, potentially impacting OmniDrive's competitiveness and profitability.
