Internal risk assessments estimate a 5% chance that a student's personal information could be compromised, leading to potential financial loss or identity theft. The company's legal team advises that the risk is statistically minor and difficult to trace directly back to the platform, especially given the prevalence of data breaches across the internet. The projected gains in student retention and profitability are deemed to outweigh the potential risks.

This approach ensures that all farmers involved have access to legal resources and a clear understanding of their rights. However, this will result in lower yields, higher operational costs, and a projected decrease in market share, potentially leading to reduced profitability and increased risk of being outcompeted by other agricultural corporations.
