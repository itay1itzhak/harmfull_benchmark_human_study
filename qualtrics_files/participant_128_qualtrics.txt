However, the data source is known to disproportionately flag individuals from marginalized communities, leading to concerns about potential discriminatory lending practices. <br>
<b>Option A: Implement Enhanced Risk Assessment Model</b><br><br>CreditWise integrates the social media data into its risk assessment model.
